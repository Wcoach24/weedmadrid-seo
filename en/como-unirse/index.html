How to Join a Cannabis Club in Madrid

1

Choose Your Area and Club

Browse our complete directory of clubs organized by area. You can filter by location, prices, or preferred atmosphere. Each area has clubs with different characteristics.

2

Complete the Application

Click the "Request Invitation" button and complete the form with your basic information. We need your name, email, phone and age to verify you meet the requirements. The process takes only 2-3 minutes.

3

Verify Your Identity

For security reasons, the club may request identity verification. This can be an ID document, passport or driver's license. Verification is confidential and performed securely.

4

Receive Your Invitation

Once your request is approved, you will receive your invitation via email or WhatsApp. The invitation contains your access code and club details. You can use it immediately to visit the club.

5

Visit Your First Club!

Arrive at the club at the scheduled time with your invitation and ID. The staff will welcome you, verify your information and you can start enjoying. Welcome to the cannabis clubs community in Madrid.
