Perguntas Frequentes sobre Clubes Cannabis em Madrid

What is a cannabis club in Madrid?

A cannabis club is a private non-profit association where adults can consume cannabis legally in a controlled setting. They operate as social clubs with membership.

What is the minimum age to enter?

Most clubs require you to be over 21 years old, although some accept members from 18. Always check with the specific club before visiting.

Do I need an invitation to enter a cannabis club?

Yes. Cannabis clubs are private associations and you need an invitation or referral from an existing member. We facilitate this process quickly and securely.

How much does a cannabis club membership cost?

Membership fees range from €20 to €50 per month depending on the club. Some offer discounted annual memberships. Product prices range from €8 to €15 per gram.

Can tourists access cannabis clubs in Madrid?

Yes, many clubs in tourist areas like Sol, Chueca and Gran Vía accept international tourists. You just need your original passport and a valid invitation.

What are the opening hours of cannabis clubs?

Most open between 12:00-14:00 and close between 22:00-01:00. Central clubs have longer hours. Fridays and Saturdays usually close later.

Can I consume inside the club?

Yes, most clubs have consumption areas with sofas, tables and a social atmosphere. This is one of the main benefits of membership. Each club has its own rules.

Is the process secure and confidential?

Absolutely. Your information is protected under Spanish data protection law (LOPD). Clubs maintain confidential records and your privacy is guaranteed.
