How to Get to Cannabis Clubs in Madrid: Complete Transport Guide

Madrid has excellent public transport that connects all cannabis clubs. This guide provides you with exact routes, metro stations, and transport options to reach clubs in each zone.

Types of Transport in Madrid

  • Metro: Fastest and most economical. 12 lines, operates 6:00 AM-1:30 AM (Friday-Saturday until 2:00 AM)
  • Bus: Good for seeing city. Slower, with frequent stops. 24h on main routes
  • Taxi/Uber: Convenient but expensive (€10-20 per trip)
  • Walking: Many clubs within walking distance of each other (e.g. Sol-Chueca: 15 minutes)

How to Buy Transport Card

  • 10-Trip Pass: €8-10 (recommended for tourists)
  • Monthly Pass: €50 (residents)
  • Daily Card: €7-9 unlimited trips in 24h

Buy at machines in any metro station or ticket windows.

Exact Routes by Zone

Cannabis Club Sol (Historic Center)

  • Direct Metro: Line 1, 2 or 3 → Stop "Sol"
  • From Atocha Station: Metro Line 1 (towards Valdecarros) → 3 stops → Sol
  • From Airport: Metro Line 8 → Chamartín → Line 1 → Sol (35 min total)
  • Exact Address: Calle del Carmen, Sol area (ask at door as member)
  • Connections: From Sol you can walk to other clubs (Chueca: 10 min, Gran Vía: 5 min)

Cannabis Club Chueca

  • Direct Metro: Line 5 → Stop "Chueca"
  • From Sol: Walk 10 minutes or take Line 3 (1 stop) → Chueca
  • From Retiro: Metro Line 2 (towards Las Rosas) → 2 stops → Banco España → Line 3 → Chueca
  • Walking from Sol: Calle Hortaleza north, 10 minutes walking

Cannabis Club Retiro

  • Direct Metro: Line 2 → Stop "Retiro"
  • From Sol: Metro Line 2 (towards Las Rosas) → 2 stops → Retiro
  • From Vallehermoso: Metro Line 1 (towards Valdecarros) → Chamberí → Line 6 → Ibiza → Line 2 (10 min total)
  • Park Access: Retiro metro exit leads directly to Retiro Park

Cannabis Club Malasaña

  • Direct Metro: Line 4 → Stop "Bilbao"
  • From Sol: Metro Line 1 (towards Pinar de Chapinería) → 2 stops → Noviciado → Line 4 (towards Argüelles) → Bilbao
  • From Chueca: Walk 10 minutes north or Metro Line 5 (towards Alameda de Osuna) → 1 stop → Bilbao
  • Bohemian Neighborhood: Area of shops and cafés. Arrive 30 min early to explore

Vallehermoso Club Social Madrid (Premium)

  • Direct Metro: Line 1 → Stop "Ríos Rosas"
  • From Sol: Metro Line 1 (towards Pinar de Chapinería) → 2 stops → Ríos Rosas
  • From Retiro: Metro Line 2 (towards Las Rosas) → Noviciado → Line 1 (towards Pinar) → Ríos Rosas
  • Residential Area: Quiet neighborhood, less touristy, near Moncayo Park

Mental Map of Connections

Main lines connecting clubs:

  • Line 1 (Red): Vallehermoso → Ríos Rosas → Noviciado → Sol → Retiro (NORTH-SOUTH AXIS)
  • Line 2 (Red): Retiro ↔ Noviciado (EAST-WEST AXIS Center)
  • Line 3 (Yellow): Sol → Chueca (DIAGONAL AXIS)
  • Line 4 (Blue): Bilbao (Malasaña) ↔ Noviciado (Center)
  • Line 5 (Green): Chueca (NORTH-SOUTH AXIS)

Recommended Metro Combinations

Sol ↔ Chueca: 10 min walking OR 1 stop metro (Line 3)

Sol ↔ Retiro: 2 stops (Line 1 or 2), 5 min

Chueca ↔ Malasaña: 10 min walking OR 1 stop (Line 5 + Line 4)

Vallehermoso ↔ Sol: 2 stops (Line 1), 7 min

Transport Tips

  • Download App: Citymapper or Google Maps for real-time routes
  • Hours: Metro runs until 1:30 AM, Friday-Saturday until 2 AM. After: taxi or 24h bus
  • Avoid Rush Hour: 8-9 AM, 1-2 PM, 5-7 PM very crowded
  • Arrive Early: Take 1 trip before (just in case)
  • Discretion: Don't post exact location on social media
  • Bring Cash: Carry cash as well as card (some taxis cash only)

From Airport to Clubs

Option 1 (Budget): Metro Line 8 (Airport) → Chamartín → Line 1 → your zone (€3 + pass, 45 min)

Option 2 (Fast): Fixed taxi (~€25-30, 30 min) or Uber (€15-25 depending on time)

Option 3 (Comfortable): Express bus Aerobus (~€5, 40 min to Atocha)

Safety on Late Night Transport

  • Travel in middle train cars (more people)
  • Guard your belongings (phone, wallet)
  • 24h bus safer than walking street late
  • Uber/Taxi recommended if arriving very late

Domande Frequenti

¿Cuál es la forma más barata de llegar a un club?

Metro con bono de 10 viajes (€8-10). Cada viaje cuesta menos de €1. Más barato que Uber/taxi (€10-20). Si no tienes tarjeta, compra bono en máquina de estación.

¿Funciona el metro de madrugada?

Metro cierra a 1:30 AM (viernes-sábado a 2 AM). Después de eso: usa autobús 24h, taxi, o Uber. Autobús es opción económica nocturna.

¿Puedo ir a pie de un club a otro?

Sí, muchos clubs están cerca. Sol-Chueca: 10 min a pie. Chueca-Malasaña: 10-15 min. Sol-Gran Vía: 5 min. Perfecto para club-hopping.

¿Es seguro el metro por la noche?

Sí, es relativamente seguro. Viaja en vagones céntricos (más gente), cuida tus cosas. Si es muy tarde, taxi es mejor opción por confort y seguridad.
