Is It Legal?

In Spain, cannabis clubs are private non-profit associations regulated by law. Although cannabis remains a controlled substance at the national level, cultivation and consumption in these clubs is tolerated by authorities as a private activity between adults. However, it is important to comply with local regulations and access requirements.

Always check local laws and verify that you meet the requirements before visiting a club.
