Tourist Guide - Cannabis Clubs in Madrid

Everything you need to know to visit a cannabis club during your stay in Madrid.

What is a Cannabis Club?

A cannabis club is a private association where adult members can legally cultivate and consume cannabis in Spain. It's not a bar or commercial store, but a private space where strict privacy and security rules are respected. Clubs operate under Spanish law and are tolerated by local authorities.

Requirements for Visitors

  • Be 18+ years old (some clubs require 21)
  • Bring valid ID (passport recommended)
  • Have invitation or be accepted as new member
  • Respect club rules

What to Expect on Your Visit

When you arrive at the club, staff will verify your identity and membership. Clubs are comfortable and safe spaces, often with consumption areas, social rooms and rest areas. Some clubs offer refreshments, snacks or a small shop. The atmosphere is generally relaxed and social among members.

Important Rules to Follow

  • No consumption of other drugs
  • You cannot bring cannabis from outside
  • Respect privacy and confidentiality of others
  • Minors are not allowed
  • Follow staff instructions

Is It Safe to Visit a Club?

Yes, cannabis clubs in Madrid are generally safe. They operate under strict rules and security systems. Clubs have identity verification protocols and maintain a controlled environment. However, as with any destination, it's always recommended to use common sense and take basic safety precautions.
